Data Deletion

How to request deletion of your application data

Requesting Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their data from our application at any time by sending an email to contact@.

Email Requirements

Please use the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account, along with any relevant identifying information that helps us locate your records securely and accurately.

What Happens Next

  • Your request will be reviewed and processed by our team.
  • Your data will be permanently deleted within a reasonable timeframe.
  • A confirmation email will be sent once deletion is complete.

Important Notes

For security and fraud-prevention reasons, we may contact you to verify ownership of the account before completing deletion. Once deletion is finalized, restoration of removed data may not be possible.
