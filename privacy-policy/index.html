Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Introduction

Vision AI Automation respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information. By using our website, applications, and related services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We May Collect

When you use our services, we may collect information that helps us deliver and improve your experience. This may include your name, email address, and account details you provide directly when contacting us or creating an account. If you authenticate through Facebook Login, we may collect basic Facebook profile data that is made available by your permissions, such as your public profile information and email address where applicable. We may also collect technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and usage activity to support platform performance, fraud prevention, and service diagnostics.

How We Use Information

We use collected data to operate our application, provide requested features, authenticate users, respond to inquiries, and maintain platform security. Information may also be used to improve functionality, troubleshoot issues, communicate relevant service updates, and comply with legal obligations. We only process data as needed to provide core service functionality and legitimate business operations.

Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect user data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel and service components that require it for operational purposes. While no system can guarantee absolute security, we continuously work to maintain appropriate safeguards and secure infrastructure practices.

Third-Party APIs and Services

Our application may integrate with third-party APIs and platforms, including Facebook/Meta services such as Facebook Login and the Facebook Graph API. When these integrations are used, your data may be processed in line with both this policy and the applicable third-party platform policies.

Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share personal data with third parties except where necessary to provide core service functionality, support integrated features you request, comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms, or protect rights, safety, and security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to request access, correction, restriction, or deletion of your data. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. We will review and respond to legitimate requests in accordance with applicable law.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like your data deleted, please submit a request by emailing contact@ with the subject line Data Deletion Request. Include the email address associated with your account and any relevant identifying information so we can verify and process your request. For full instructions, visit our Data Deletion page.

Contact

If you have privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us at contact@.
